**Recently, I had the privilege of delivering my first public training session at the 2024 Global Power Platform Bootcamp where I gave an introduction to the Power Query Advanced Editor and the M Language. I've included a recording of the training below.**

Making the leap from the Power Query UI to using the Advanced Editor can be intimidating, especially for those who aren't used to looking at code. Helping to bridge that gap, this training demonstrates that Advanced Editor is not as scary as it seems and that you can do a lot of amazing things with a little knowledge of M.
